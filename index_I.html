    What is dessert?

    Term used to designate some sweet preparation that is usually served at the end of the meal. In Mexico there are many sweets or fruit pastes that serve to finish a meal. the desserts are used as desserts during weddings, baptisms or birthdays. However, htey are not very common in daily food.
